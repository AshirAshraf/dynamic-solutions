About Us

Dynamic Solutions specializes in Fire and Security solutions and are committed to meet the needs of its customers by providing the means of obtaining safety and security in daily life. We provide a range of products and services that include the supply, design, installation and maintenance of Passive and Active Fire protection, Smoke and Natural Ventilation systems, Fire alarm systems (including detection and control devices) and Security Management Systems that include Access Control, CCTV and other security systems.
